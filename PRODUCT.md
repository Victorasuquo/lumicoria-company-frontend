**Register:** brand

## What it is

Lumicoria.com is the enterprise delivery, consulting, and applied research arm of Lumicoria Inc. It designs, deploys, and operates production AI agents on Lumicoria.ai for businesses that want a measurable outcome without building an internal agent platform.

## Primary audience

- Founders and CEOs at 10-200 person B2B companies.
- COOs and operations leaders at 50-500 person organisations.
- Revenue and customer leaders with a named workflow bottleneck.
- Technical leaders who need delivery capacity and enterprise governance.

## Primary conversion

Book a discovery call by email. The initial conversation maps one workflow, its current cost, the systems involved, and the success metric.
