# Lumicoria.com

**Register:** brand

**Surface in focus:** company and agency website

## What it is

Lumicoria.com is the enterprise delivery, consulting, and applied research arm of Lumicoria Inc. It designs, deploys, and operates production AI agents on Lumicoria.ai for businesses that want a measurable outcome without building an internal agent platform.

## Primary audience

- Founders and CEOs at 10-200 person B2B companies.
- COOs and operations leaders at 50-500 person organisations.
- Revenue and customer leaders with a named workflow bottleneck.
- Technical leaders who need delivery capacity and enterprise governance.

## Primary conversion

Start a conversation by email. The first call maps one workflow, its current cost, the systems involved, the human decisions inside it, and the success metric.

## Core offers

- Discovery and Design Sprint: 2-4 weeks, $7.5k-$15k.
- Production Agent Build: 6-10 weeks, $35k-$85k.
- Workflow Orchestration: 12-20 weeks, $90k-$200k.
- Optimisation Retainer: $4k-$25k per month.

## Voice

Clear, human, confident, and outcome-first. Explain recognizable business work before platform architecture. No hype, fake case studies, vague AI claims, or invented proof.

## Homepage narrative

- Make the business promise clear before describing the technology.
- Let buyers recognize their own work across sales, service, operations, and people workflows.
- Explain why Lumicoria combines a delivery practice with its own platform.
- Show a narrow, responsible path from first workflow to production.
- Keep pricing, human controls, applied research, and common objections visible.

## Proof available today

- Every engagement runs on Lumicoria.ai.
- 21 production agents in the platform library.
- Six model providers with routing and fallback.
- Three supported vector-store backends.
- A documented six-week production delivery framework.
