# Lumicoria.com Design Direction

## Design read

A proof-led company site for executive operators evaluating an AI delivery partner. The visual language is calm, engineered, and materially confident.

## Dials

- Design variance: 7.
- Motion intensity: 6.
- Visual density: 4.

## Visual system

- Space Grotesk for display and body, preserving the Lumicoria brand guide.
- Space Mono only for system labels and technical signals.
- Lumicoria violet is a committed brand surface, supported by violet-tinted neutrals, Signal Clarity blue, and Outcome Gold.
- No gradients, gradient text, decorative glows, generic glass cards, or fake product screenshots.
- Cards use 14px corners. Buttons and the floating navigation use full pills.

## Page architecture

1. Outcome-led hero and six-week production blueprint.
2. Platform proof rail.
3. Interactive workflow outcome explorer.
4. Adjustable workflow capacity estimator.
5. Interactive six-week delivery system.
6. Transparent productised engagement paths.
7. Production governance and decision-control matrix.
8. Applied research practice.
9. Discovery-call close.

## Motion

- Hero load establishes hierarchy.
- In-view reveals support narrative pacing.
- Tabs and timeline transitions explain state changes.
- All motion respects reduced-motion preferences.
